Still thinking it over?

Every question we get asked before a client says yes — answered honestly

Do I need to pay monthly?

No. You pay once for the build and own the system forever. Monthly support is optional — add it if you want us to monitor, update, and optimize your system. Cancel anytime.

What do I pay directly to third parties?

WhatsApp API usage (~$0.04–0.08 per conversation, charged by Meta), AI API calls (~$1–20/mo for most businesses), and any tools you already use (CRM, Google Workspace). Most small businesses pay $5–30/mo in API costs.

Can I upgrade from Starter to Growth later?

Yes. We build everything to be expandable. You pay the difference — we add the new jobs on top of what's already running. Nothing gets rebuilt from scratch.

What if something breaks after the 14-day support?

If you're on a support plan, we fix it at no extra cost. If you're not, we offer one-time fix packages — or you can add the monthly plan at any time.

I'm not technical. Will I understand how to use it?

Yes. We build the system and train you. You don't need to touch any code. Most clients are fully confident within one training session.

How do I know which plan is right for me?

Book a free 30-min audit. We'll ask about your business, find the most painful daily task, and tell you exactly what we'd automate first — no pressure, no commitment.
